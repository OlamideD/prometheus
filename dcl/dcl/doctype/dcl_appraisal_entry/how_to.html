Rating Standards
1. Unsatisfactory - Work performance is inadequate and inferior to the standards of performance required for the position. Performance at this level cannot be allowed to continue.
2. Needs improvement - Work performance does not consistently meet the standards of performance for the position. Serious effort is needed to improve performance.
3. Meets job requirements - Work performance consistently meets the standards of performance for the position.
4. Exceeds job requirements - Work performance is consistently above the standard performance for the position. 5. Outstanding - Work performance consistently superior to standards required for the job.
